Denver's Permitting and Licensing Center


The City & County of Denver is in the process of updating its payment platform to a more modern, secure solution.
Please note: if your company has debit filters in place on the bank account used to make EFT payments to the City, this Company ID will change effective December 9, 2025. The new Company ID is 9990008222. If this company ID is not updated with your bank, EFTs may be returned "Not Authorized", and payment will need to be reissued accordingly.


All Permitting Customers: The City & County of Denver charges a 2.5% service fee on eligible credit and debit card transactions to help offset the City’s cost of processing these transactions. The service fee is non-refundable. This change will take effect for all ePermit online customers on December 9, 2025.
There are no fees for payments online by electronic check or for in-person payments via cash, check or money order.

Welcome to Denver's online permitting and licensing center!


  • Development Services: Apply or pay for development and construction permits, Check plan review status, Schedule inspections, Search permit records
  • Business Licenses: Apply, renew, or modify a business license, pay license or public health citations, search license and public health records
  • Contractor Licensing: Apply for or renew a license or certificate
  • Right-of-Way: Apply for a ROW permit, Request an address, Schedule a ROW inspection, Search ROW permit records
  • Conveyance:  Apply for and maintain conveyance certificates, Search records, change contacts or renew a certificate and operational permit
  • Fire: Apply for operational permits, Search records.  Denver Fire Department

Contractors: After logging in, go to "My Account" to add your license.

Need help? Dial 3-1-1 within Denver, (720) 913-1311 from outside of Denver or use our online E-permits tutorials, videos, and FAQs.
